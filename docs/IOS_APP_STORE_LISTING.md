## App Store Description

Transform your recipe collection with Velvet Ladle.

Velvet Ladle is your personal recipe manager for collecting, organizing, and finding recipes quickly. Save recipes from websites, add family recipes manually, and keep your favorites in one place.

Key Features

- Smart recipe import from websites
  - Extract ingredients, directions, and nutrition details
  - Automatically capture images and cooking times
  - Manual entry for handwritten or family recipes

- Powerful search and filtering
  - Search by recipe title, ingredients, or description
  - Filter by cuisine and ingredient combinations
  - Fast access to recently viewed and favorite recipes

- Nutrition and timing insights
  - Per-serving nutrition summary (calories, protein, carbs, fat, and more)
  - Prep, cook, and total time display
  - Helpful nutrition tips on each recipe

- Favorites and organization
  - Save and manage favorite recipes
  - Keep your personal collection easy to browse
  - Edit and update recipes anytime

- Account and sync support
  - Free account includes secure cloud backup and sync
  - Guest mode lets users try the app before signing up
  - Works without complex setup

Free and Premium

- Free account: save up to 10 recipes with full edit/delete support
- Premium subscription: unlimited recipe storage and future premium features

Why Velvet Ladle

Velvet Ladle focuses on practical recipe management without clutter. If your recipes are scattered across bookmarks, screenshots, and notes, Velvet Ladle gives you one clean place to manage everything.
